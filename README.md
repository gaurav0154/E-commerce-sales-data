# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI / Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **E-Commerce Sales Data** to uncover insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of **sales, profit, orders, and regional performance**, helping businesses make data-driven decisions to improve revenue and efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance  
- Track total orders and quantity sold  
- Identify high-performing categories and regions  
- Monitor profit margins and discount impact  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Provide actionable business insights  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)

- **Total Sales:** $2.29M  
- **Total Profit:** $286.4K  
- **Total Orders:** 9994 
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 37,873  
- **Profit Margin:** 12.47%  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Sales & Profit Analysis

- Monthly breakdown of sales and profit  
- Identification of high and low-performing months  
- Seasonal trends in revenue generation  

---

### 2. Category & Sub-Category Analysis

- Performance across product categories  
- Identification of top-selling and low-performing products  
- Helps optimize product strategy  

---

### 3. Regional Analysis (Map View)

- Sales distribution across regions and states  
- Identifies high-revenue geographic areas  
- Helps in regional marketing strategies  

---

### 4. Top Performing Products

- Top 5 products based on sales and profit  
- Helps in inventory and promotion decisions  

---

### 5. Profitability Analysis

- Tracks profit vs sales relationship  
- Identifies loss-making transactions  
- Impact of discounts on profitability  

---

### 6. Customer & Order Insights

- Total unique orders: 9994
- Customer purchasing patterns  
- Order volume trends over time  

---

### 7. Year-over-Year (YoY) Analysis

- Comparison of sales growth across years  
- Helps track business growth trends
